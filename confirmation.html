Refund Policy

Last updated: July 2025

1. Eligibility for Refund

You may request a refund under the following conditions:

  • The order was not delivered within 24 hours of payment.
  • The delivered product is incorrect or does not match the ordered item.
  • The order could not be fulfilled due to a technical error on our end.
  • Duplicate payment was made for the same order.

2. Non-Refundable Items

Refunds will NOT be issued in the following cases:

  • Incorrect account ID, game ID, or server details provided by the customer.
  • The account is banned, suspended, or restricted by the game/service provider.
  • Digital items that have already been delivered and used/consumed.
  • Gift cards or PIN codes that have been revealed or redeemed.
  • Change of mind after the order has been processed.

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us through one of the following channels:

  • Email: support.ilhamstore@gmail.com
  • Telegram: @official_Ilhamstore
  • Messenger: IlhamStore Messenger

Please include your order ID, payment method, and reason for the refund request.

4. Refund Processing Time

  • bKash / Nagad / Rocket / Upay: 1–3 business days after approval.
  • Store Credit: Instant after approval.

Refunds will be processed using the same payment method used for the original purchase whenever possible.

5. Partial Refunds

In some cases, a partial refund may be offered if:

  • Only part of the order was delivered.
  • The service was significantly delayed but eventually completed.

6. Delivery Issues

If you experience delivery issues:

  • Contact support within 48 hours of placing the order.
  • Provide a screenshot of your order confirmation and any error messages.
  • Our team will investigate and resolve the issue, or issue a refund if the order cannot be fulfilled.

7. Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with a refund decision, you may escalate the matter by emailing support.ilhamstore@gmail.com with detailed information. We will review and respond within 5 business days.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does top-up work?

Simply select the product you want, choose a package, enter your game/account ID, and complete payment. Your top-up will be delivered to your account automatically or manually within minutes.

How long does delivery take?

Most orders are delivered instantly or within 5–15 minutes. Some orders may take up to 24 hours depending on server status, game maintenance, or high demand periods. You will be notified if there are any delays.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bKash, Nagad, Rocket, and Upay. Simply complete the payment using your preferred method and submit the transaction ID for verification.

Is my payment secure?

Yes. Our website uses SSL encryption, and we process payments through trusted and secure gateways. Your financial information is never stored on our servers.

What if I don't receive my top-up?

If you don't receive your order within 24 hours, please contact our support team immediately via Telegram or Messenger with your order details. We will investigate and resolve the issue or provide a refund.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are available for orders that could not be delivered. Please read our Refund Policy for full details on eligibility and the refund process.

Do I need an account to order?

No account registration is required. Simply select your product, enter the required details, make payment, and receive your top-up. However, providing accurate information is essential for successful delivery.

I entered the wrong ID. What should I do?

Please contact us immediately before the order is processed. If the order has already been delivered to the wrong account, we may not be able to reverse it. Always double-check your ID before confirming payment.

How do I track my order?

You can use the Track Order feature in the header of our website. Enter your order ID to check the current status of your order.

How can I contact support?

  • Email: support.ilhamstore@gmail.com
  • Telegram: @official_Ilhamstore
  • Messenger: IlhamStore Messenger
  • Facebook: Official Page

Do you offer discounts or promotions?

Yes! Follow our Facebook page and Telegram channel for the latest deals, discounts, and promotional offers.
